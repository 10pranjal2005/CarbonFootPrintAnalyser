Tips to Reduce Your Footprint

  • Reduce meat and dairy consumption.
  • Opt for public transport, biking, or walking.
  • Switch to energy-efficient appliances and LED lighting.
  • Minimize air travel, especially short-haul flights.
  • Reduce, reuse, and recycle. Avoid single-use plastics.
