About Carbon Wise

This Carbon Footprint Calculator is a personalized tool designed to help individuals track and understand their environmental impact on a quarterly basis.

Methodology

The calculations are based on a set of standardized emission factors for various activities. By inputting your quarterly data, the application provides an estimate of your carbon footprint in kilograms of CO₂ equivalent (kg CO₂e). The goal is to provide awareness and insights that can help you make more informed, sustainable choices.
